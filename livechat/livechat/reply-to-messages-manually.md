# Reply to messages manually

To be able to reply to messages manually, you can press the **Join room** button in the AI Virtual Assistant section to join the room

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (78).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Join room button at AI Sales Assistant chats section</p></figcaption></figure>

or you can click the **Join room** button in the conversation box

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (79).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Join room button in conversation box</p></figcaption></figure>

After clicking **Join room** -> the system moves the joined conversation to the My Chats section and allows you to enter the content of the conversation with the customer.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (80).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Joined room - allows entering message content</p></figcaption></figure>

As for the current state<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (81).png" alt="" data-size="line">, the chatbot still responds in parallel with you.&#x20;

If you want to stop the chatbot's response, click **Pause** Virtual Agent in the conversation box, the system changes the status to<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (82).png" alt="" data-size="line">, and turns off the active status.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (83).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Chatbot status is inactive</p></figcaption></figure>

If you want to exit the room so the chatbot can continue to answer automatically, press the  Exit button in the conversation box

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (84).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Exit button in the conversation box</p></figcaption></figure>

The system moves conversation to AI Sales Assistant Chats section
