# Remove broadcast

You can delete a created Broadcast by pressing the **Delete** button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (918).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The system displays a popup confirming deletion, click **OK** to delete the broadcast

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (919).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click <strong>OK</strong> to remove the created broadcast from the system</p></figcaption></figure>
