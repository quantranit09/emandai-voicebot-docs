# Forgot password

Retrieve the password of the account you want to access by clicking the **Forgot password** button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (873).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Forgot password screen</p></figcaption></figure>

Enter the username you want to retrieve the password and press **Confirm** button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (874).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Username entry screen</p></figcaption></figure>

After clicking confirm, the system sends an email to the email address used to register for the account

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (875).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Email change password</p></figcaption></figure>

Click on **Change password** -> the system navigates to **Reset password** screen allowing users to enter the new password

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (876).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Reset password screen</p></figcaption></figure>

Enter the new password you want to adjust and click on **Confirm** button
