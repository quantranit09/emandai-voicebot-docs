# Preview

The Preview function allows users to:

* Use the created AI Assistant without logging in&#x20;
* Send users a trial of the Assistant AI created&#x20;

At the AI Assistant Test screen, tap the Preview button to access Preview mode

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (50).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Press the Preview button to access Preview mode</p></figcaption></figure>

The link can be shared with other users by tapping the **Share** button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (51).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Press the Share button to copy preview link</p></figcaption></figure>

You can listen to the chat again as the AI assistant responds

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (52).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Press the Play button to play the message</p></figcaption></figure>

Press the button <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (53).png" alt="" data-size="line"> to play the chat responded by the bot&#x20;

While playing, having a status <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (54).png" alt="" data-size="line"> -> Press to stop playing



Ask the bot a question/problem by voice by pressing the **Send a voice clip** button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (55).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Press Send a voice clip to send a voice message</p></figcaption></figure>

You can listen to the recording again

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (56).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
