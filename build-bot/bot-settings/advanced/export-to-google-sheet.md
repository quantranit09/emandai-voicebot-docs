# Export to Google Sheet

**Export to Google Sheet**

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (899).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Exporting Google Sheet information is a feature that allows the system to automatically transfer collected information into a Google sheet file&#x20;

Default: this feature will be enabled. If you click on the toggle icon, the system will disable this function&#x20;

When activating the feature, collected information will be sent to the email address used to register for the account

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (900).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click the Update button to save the changed information</p></figcaption></figure>

After installation -> click the **Update** button to save the changed information
