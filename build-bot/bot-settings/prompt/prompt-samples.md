# Prompt samples

To customize your prompt to match your business needs, you can utilize prompt samples as a reference.

In the system, we have created and shared pre-defined prompt samples for various business fields, allowing users to review and select the appropriate prompt.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (142).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on checkbox of the selected prompt sample -> click <strong>Apply</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

To select a suitable prompt sample, tick the checkbox next to it. After selecting the checkbox, click on **Apply**. The system will then display the content of the selected prompt on the **Job Description**. Users can further edit the displayed content to match their business requirements.

The user can click on <img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (465).png" alt="" data-size="line"> to view the selected prompt in detail and illustrated images of how the Bot response

Moreover, you can refer to the list of prompt samples prepared by the development team by clicking on <img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (725).png" alt="" data-size="line">, you can copy the prompt sample that suits your business requirements and apply it to the **Job description**
