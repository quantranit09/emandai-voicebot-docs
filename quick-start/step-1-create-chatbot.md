Click on the 'Create AI Chatbot' button to display the **'Create AI Assistant'** pop-up.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (192).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>The <strong>'Create AI Assistant'</strong> pop-up</p></figcaption></figure>

At this step, you need to fill in two fields of information:

* When creating a new chatbot, it is important to provide a unique chatbot name that has not been previously used in the system.
* Chatbot description: This section provides a brief overview of the Chatbot's purpose and scope of activities, as well as any additional notes the user wishes to add.

After entering the information and clicking **Create**, the pop-up will be closed, and the newly created bot will appear on the homepage.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (194).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>List of Bots created in the system</p></figcaption></figure>

**Action options: Edit and Remove ChatBot**

At the Chatbot List screen, for each specific Chatbot, there will be an **Options** menu, allowing users to quickly perform **Edit** and **Remove** Chatbot.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (195).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Options</strong> menu</p></figcaption></figure>

Click on **Edit** -> the system will display the **Edit chatbot** pop-up that allows users to edit the created chatbot information: chatbot name and chatbot description

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (196).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Edit chatbot</strong> pop-up</p></figcaption></figure>

Click **Remove** -> the system will display the **Remove Chatbot** pop-up, you need to click **Confirm** for the system to operate to delete the created bot

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (197).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Remove Chatbot</strong> pop-up</p></figcaption></figure>

From the list of created Chatbots, click on the specific Chatbot, and the system will navigate to the Bot Settings screen.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (198).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>The Bot Settings screen</p></figcaption></figure>
