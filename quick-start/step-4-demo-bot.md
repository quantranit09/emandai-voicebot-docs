# Step 4 - Demo bot

Once you complete Knowledge Base, choose **Demo AI Assistant** on the menu bar on the left side of the screen

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (686).png" alt=""><figcaption><p> <strong>Demo AI Assistant</strong> screen</p></figcaption></figure>

This step will allow you to have hands-on experience of interacting with chatbots

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (687).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Type content to ask bot here</p></figcaption></figure>

Enter content at **Type here...** To ask questions for the bot, it's recommended to ask questions related to the information in the Knowledge Base. This way, you can confirm that the bot's response is accurate and uses the correct data to answer your question. You can expect a response from the bot within seconds, and it will be displayed in the chat dialog.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (688).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Conversation interface</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (689).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Data source Bot used from the Knowledge Base</p></figcaption></figure>

You can click on the References link, to navigate to the Knowledge Base screen to review the imported document.

The above are the basic steps to quickly create a Chatbot. To set up a smarter and more optimal Chatbot, please refer the guide on Building a Chatbot
