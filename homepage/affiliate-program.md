# Affiliate Program

_**Before becoming a VAgent partner, you must have an account to access VAgent**_\
[Register for VAgent now!](https://vagent.ai/#/auth/register)

***

To become a VAgent partner, you have to:

* On the User Profile, click **Join Affiliate**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (773).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click to display the popup to become a VAgent partner</p></figcaption></figure>

Registration interface to become a VAgent partner

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (774).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Popup becomes a partner of VAgent</p></figcaption></figure>

Information needed to become a VAgent partner:&#x20;

* ID number
* ID card front&#x20;
* ID card back&#x20;
* Place of issues&#x20;
* Date of issues&#x20;
* Address&#x20;
* Tax code (if any)

Click **Join now** to send the filled information to VAgent

***

After successful registration, the Dashboard screen adds an **Affiliate** section

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (775).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Affiliate -> the system displays Affiliate program information</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (111).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliate program screen</p></figcaption></figure>

The Affiliate Program helps you introduce VAgent to other users and earn commissions based on the revenue of the person you refer. Simply copy and share the link to the people you want to refer here:

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (112).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click copy and share the link</p></figcaption></figure>

You can see your membership type here:

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (113).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Member type: <strong>Partner</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (863).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Member type: <strong>Collaborator</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Click on **See more about Affiliate policy**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (114).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click to see more about Affiliate policy</p></figcaption></figure>

The system displays general statistical parameters, such as:

* Number of visitors clicking on the shared link and the growth rate compared to the previous month

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (115).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Number of visitors clicking on the shared link</p></figcaption></figure>

* Number of new registered customers following the shared link and the growth rate compared to the previous month

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (116).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Number of new registered customers following the shared link</p></figcaption></figure>

* Number of customers who purchased the package using the shared link and the growth rate compared to the previous month

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (117).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Number of customers purchasing the package using the shared link</p></figcaption></figure>

* Total revenue of customers purchasing the package according to the shared link and the growth rate compared to the previous month

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (118).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Total revenue of customers purchasing the package according to the shared link</p></figcaption></figure>

**Activity chart**
